About Us

Hello I'm Fatmanur Barcın
I'm from Turkey and 25 years
I'm doing my doctorate degree student of Eötvös Loránd University.
Researcher and Author Fatma Nur Barcın, I have tried to synthesize activities that can be used for family and children to spend productive time by talking with children from different geographies, producing cognitive, emotional and behavioral games in order to develop the excitement of my own childhood, their interests and the endless childish spirit and to explore the childhood period better . I am currently doing my master's degree in Educational Sciences in Hungary. I decided to establish this platform where I can convey my thoughts to you in order to share my experiences during my master degree with you, to be a source of inspiration for parents and to benefit from the experiences of those seeking advice in the field of child development. Finally I decided to take a mission and do it consciously. I started writing about my thoughts that emerged from my research and experiences, and suggestions that would be stimulating for developmental adults for children, especially as an observer from the world of children aged 4-6. I set up the childhood educator platform with the mission of sharing my thoughts with others. You can spend some time exploring the blog and learning interesting things,
you can contact me if you want to collaborate on any project.
